21 Best E-commerce Websites of Nepal

We are in the digital world and in this era, we can do just about everything online. This naturally includes shopping as well. Although it may seem a bit hard to believe it has been more than two decades since the first e-commerce website was registered in Nepal. It all started with Thamel.com back in 2000. 

Although not the most prominent e-commerce website in modern times, the first e-commerce website of Nepal is still doing well. But what about the e-commerce website that is at the top? 

Top E-commerce Websites in Nepal

Nepal has progressed a lot since the earlier days of an e-commerce website design and development. At this point, we have thousands of e-commerce websites in Nepal. As such, it can be a bit hard to know the best e-commerce websites in Nepal. 

That said, there are a few e-commerce websites that we know are among the best. Some of these names include Sastodeal and Hamrobazar. And indeed these names are among the best e-commerce websites in Nepal. But what about other e-commerce websites?

Well, this is exactly what we will be covering today. So without any further ado, let's get started with the list. 

Note* The ranking on this list will be based on the Alexa Rank Nepal at the time of this writing. 

1) Daraz

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 8 
  • Category: Multi-Vendor 
  • Mobile App: Yes

Daraz is undoubtedly one of the top e-commerce sites in Nepal. This is a renowned online commerce shopping site that was launched back in 2012. With thousands of visitors per day and hundreds of purchases, this website does indeed deserve to be called the best multi-vendor e-commerce website in Nepal. 

2) HamroBazar

  • Alexa Rank Nepal:46
  • Category: Multi-Vendor
  • Mobile App: Yes

Hamrobazar is a well-known online shopping portal and this website in Nepal is well known for enabling just about everyone to buy and sell products. It's easy to use, and user-friendly and if you browse long enough you can find some hilarious products here.

3) Thulo

  • Alexa Rank Nepal:164
  • Category: Multi Vendor
  • Mobile App: Yes

As its name suggests thulo is a big e-commerce website that offers you a wide range of products on just about everything you need. This includes everything from a variety of products on personnel health to fashion ware and electronics. 

4) Sastodeal

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 209
  • Category: Muti vendor 
  • Mobile App: Yes

As the name suggests sastodeal is a place where you can find everything you need at a discount rate. Here, you can find every product you need from grocery items to books and electronics. This website has all the potential to rise in rank in near future and with a bit of time and a minor change in trend, it can very well reach the top of this list. 

5) Dealayo

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 467
  • Category: Multi Vendor
  • Mobile App: No

Although this online shopping site does not have mobile applications, it is still among the best e-commerce platforms in Nepal. Similar to sastodeal, this website is all about providing the best deals on everyday needs and electronics. 

6) OkDam

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 578
  • Category: Electronics and Hardware
  • Mobile App: yes

OkDam covers a bit narrower niche when compared to the rest of the online shopping portals. However, it is one of the best e-commerce sites in Nepal. They have some of the best electronics and hardware in Nepal along with fashion and a variety of products on health. 

7) Foodmandu

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 864
  • Category: Food
  • Mobile App: Yes

As its name suggests this e-commerce website is all about food. You can order the best foods from a wide range menu at your convenience and affordable prices. 

8) Smartdoko

  • Alexa Rank Nepal:1006
  • Category: Multi-Vendor
  • Mobile App: Yes

Smartdoko is another e-commerce site that has just about everything you need. This website is simple to use, has a wide range of products to choose from, and has an easy sorting system. As with others, with the right turn of trend, this online shopping site in Nepal can easily reach the top. 

9) Gyapu

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 1065
  • Category: Multi-vendor
  • Mobile App: Yes

Gyapu is just everything that we have mentioned but with the added benefit of online rewards. Along with the usual services of a wide range of product choices, you can also be a seller easily. 

10) Socheko 

  • Alexa Rank Nepal:1273
  • Category: Lifestyle and appliances
  • Mobile App: Yes

Socheko is an e-commerce platform that has a relatively narrower niche. Another thing that separates Socheko from other online shopping portals is that Socheko does not promise great prices but rather great quality. 

11) Mero Kirana

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 1946
  • Category: Grocery and lifestyle
  • Mobile App: Yes

Like its name suggests this e-commerce website is all about local grocery goods. You can find just about everything for daily life and this e-commerce site in Nepal is just an online version of your local mega grocery store.

12) Bhojdeals

  • Alexa Rank Nepal:2529
  • Category: Grocery and food
  • Mobile App: Yes

Bhojdeals is an e-commerce website in Nepal that provides us with the best deals on food and grocery. Although at the time of this writing Bhojdeals is a bit lower on the list, it just needs a small push and the right trend to be one of the best. 

13)  Onlinesathi

  • Alexa Rank Nepal:3249
  • Category: Multi Vendor
  • Mobile App: No

If we exclude a bit of rawness from this e-commerce website, it is indeed a decent website. However, since this e-commerce site is still in its early phase there is still a lot of room to grow. That said, with these small exceptions, it has a wide range of products and you can find just about everything here. 

14) Muncha

  • Alexa Rank Nepal: 3275
  • Category: Multi-vendor
  • Mobile App: No

Muncha is a good e-commerce website and it is another one on this list that is still in its earlier phase. That said, this e-commerce website has already gained a decent following and is a popular choice for purchasing gifting products. 

15) Meroshopping

  • Alexa Rank Nepal:
  • Category: Multi-Vendor
  • Mobile App: Yes (Beta)

This is one of the most popular e-commerce platforms in Nepal that is oddly lower on this list. This e-commerce website has a decent amount of products, is easy to use and all it needs is the right timing to reach the top in this list of the best e-commerce sites in Nepal.

Honorable Mentions

Not all popular e-commerce websites get a ranking on Alexa. That said, just because an e-commerce website is not listed on Alexa does not mean that it is not good. As such, the following is the list of popular and good e-commerce sites that is does not have a national ranking.

16) Bigpasal

Bigpasal is an online shopping website that deals with smartphones and accessories. This e-commerce popular online shopping site does not have mobile applications. 

17) Dokoman

Dokoman is a relatively recent electronics and home appliances e-commerce platform with a well-received mobile app on playstore. 

18) Mynepshop

This online shopping website is popular among netizens who like to send gifts via online portals. 

19) Gajabko

Gajabko is another popular online shopping site with a variety of products. It does not have a mobile app. 

20) Bhakari

Bhakari is a popular online shopping website for purchasing electronics and appliances. This website does not have a mobile application.

21) Vegmandu

This popular e-commerce website is all about providing the best deals for online shopping of vegetables and fruits. 

Conclusion

Nepal is a growing nation and it has come a long way since its first e-commerce website back in 2000. Now, we have hundreds of e-commerce sites, so much so that many are just waiting to be ranked. While this list is indeed composed of some of the best e-commerce websites in Nepal, you should note that this ranking can change at any time.
