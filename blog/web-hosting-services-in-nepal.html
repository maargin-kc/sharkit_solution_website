FAQ: What is the Web Hosting Price in Nepal?

This is one of the most frequent questions regarding web hosting in Nepal. But the sad part is that there is no specific value that can be said as the web hosting price in Nepal. The simplest and best answer to this question is, “it depends on your needs and the services provided by the web hosting company”.

The price range depends on the type of website you want to host and the services you want within the website and from the web host. It can be done as cheaply as 10 to 15K, or the price can range to a few lakhs. The more you spend on the services in a well-trusted company, the better your web service is. That being said, you don’t have to choose the ones with extensive services at extreme cost. As long as the web host has services that fit your need, it’s all good. 

For instance, if you just need to host a website with a preformatted design without any content, the price will be lower since the web development doesn’t need to do much work. But if you need a website that has a fully customized design along with supreme audio/visual UI/UX design and great content, then you should be ready to shed anywhere from a few ten thousand to a few lakhs. 

The price of web hosting depends on what you need. But while going for a web host, be sure to get what you need and nothing more. You won’t need a complex UI if your website is just for news and a few blogs.

Just be wary of the quacks who promise the services but don’t deliver after making the payment.
