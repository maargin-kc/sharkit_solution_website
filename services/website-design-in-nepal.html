How Do We Work

Goal and Scope Definition

Our experts take in your input, analyze your needs and figure out the scope. After taking in your input, we understand your goal and define the scope of your website and the business connected with it. 

Wireframe creation 

Once the goal and scope have been defined, our experts create the visual prototype of your website for verification. 

Website Building

After verifying the prototype of the website, our team of expert programmers will create your website as per your needs. This is where the core of your website is created. 

Content Creation and Visual Elements Design

A website is not just about codes; it also consists of design elements and content. The design elements and content will vary depending on your website type. With our team of experts, Softbenz provides you with the best content and interactive design elements per your need. 

Testing and Debugging

Once all the coding and content are done, the next phase is to test how well your website works and remove any potential bugs. 

Launching and Monitoring

Once every process is completed, the final step is to launch the website and monitor its performance. Depending on the status and performance of the launched website, we will provide you with further changes and upgrades as required. 

FAQ

1) What are website development services?

In layman's terms, website development services are intended to provide you with a customized website per your needs. These services cover a wide field ranging from simple and static website design to customized and dynamic websites that match your brand.

2) Why does my company need a website?

If your company has a website, it will take advantage of many business opportunities. More than half of the world relies on the internet, and having a website gives you exposure to these people and provides you with better business opportunities. Besides, your competitor probably has one as well, and you will need one to stay relevant and in competition in the modern business world. 

3) Do you create WordPress websites?

Yes, we do develop WordPress websites alongside our custom websites, including e-commerce websites, business websites, and more.

4) Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Our websites are mobile-friendly and cross-platform, and accessible from any web browser.

5) How many pages do I get with my website?

The number of web pages will be based on your requirement. Regardless of the number, your requirements will be fulfilled.

6) What is the cost of website development offered by Softbenz?

We offer a wide range of web development packages. These packages range from basic ones with standard features to enterprise packages with premium features. We also provide custom packages, where your website's cost will be based on your needs. For further details, please check our web development packages or let us know by contacting us. 

7) How long will it take to develop a website?

The time taken for website development will vary depending on the type and complexity of a website. Generally, it takes around two to four months to design and build an average website, depending on the requirements. 

8) Do you provide domain registration and website hosting services as well?

Yes, we do. Along with website development, we provide domain registration and web hosting services for our clients as per their request. 

9) Who will write content for the website?

Softbenz has a team of expert content and SEO experts for your website content. Our expert team members provide your website with quality content through a combined effort regardless of your needs. 

10) What kind of website do you build?

We develop all kinds of websites like:

  • Business Websites
