Career

Graphics and Motion Graphics

Functional Title: Marketing member

Corporate Title: Graphic Designer

No. of Vacancies: 1

Posted date : Dec 29, 2023

Requirements
    • Bachelor degree in Computer, Engineering or a related subject.
    • minimum of 2 years of experience in the same field.
    • Conceptualize and develop logos.
    • Design and creative concepts for websites that tell a compelling story about how products or services can aid their business needs.
    • Work closely with Marketing partners to support strategic marketing initiatives and deliver projects within the agreed-upon timeframe.
    • Recommend design solutions that are both versatile and on-brand.
    • Research and recommend implementation of current design trends and techniques.
    • Design and produce various marketing communication materials across several media
    • Should be able to make animations and videos.
    • Strong passion for digital art and graphic design
    • Excellent communication skills, both written and oral
    • Ability to work independently and in a collaborative team environment
Skills Desired
    • Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and sketch.
    • After Effects, Premiere Pro, or any other software for motion graphics
Key Responsibilities
    • Social media post Design 
    • Logo Design/Redesign
    • Videos/GIF for Facebook Instagram and other social sites
    • Banner design for website 

    Note: Your responsibilities are not just limited to the above-mentioned points. Might have to work according to the requirement of the clients.

Benefits
    • Salary: Negotiable (Depending upon your skills and experience).
